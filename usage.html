Unacceptable Usage

Unacceptable scale. Logo can't be scale in any way.
Unacceptable coloring. Logo can be colored only in official colours or in black-and-white.
Unacceptable skew. The logo should be striat, all the elements should be symmeric. Logo can't have any skew.
Unacceptable bluring. Logo image should always be sharp and readable, can't be blur.
Unacceptable rotation. Logo can have only one position with the star on the top and can't have any rotation.
Unacceptable filter usage. It's unacceptable to use any kind of filters on the logo, as it ruin whole colour scheme.
