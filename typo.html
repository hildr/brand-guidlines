Brand Typography

There are two official typefaces, which the club using for it's branding: Arial and Roboto. Arial is a primiry typeface of the brand, it's using on the badge design and on the web-site, as a font family for all the headers. Roboto is a secondary typeface, using to represent all the information on the web-sire or others promitional items.
