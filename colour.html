There are 6 colours of the club badge. 4 of the are primary colours: Red, Blue, Yellow and White; and also two secondary colors: Black and White-Yellow. All the colours represented in the scheme using the circle shape(shape of the ball).
For the printing production should be used CMYK color version.
For the digital production (screen, web-sites, mobile app. and etc) should be used RGB color version.
