Club Mascot

The Steed, one of the main symbols of PFC CSKA club and also the nick name of the team, now officially has become PFC CSKA Mascot. By its shape and colors it matches the club logo. The mascot was created by FIRMA studio, the official kit and logo designer for PFC CSKA.
Now PFC CSKA is the only Russian club with official mascot. Further development of the club attributes will help PFC CSKA to become a well-known international brand.
